WHAT IS PROJECT INVICTUS:

This piece is a collaboration between SEISHI International and CALIBEARZ, merging the worlds of calisthenics and fashion. At its core is the word “INVICTUS”, derived from Latin, meaning unconquerable or undefeated - a powerful symbol of resilience and strength in the face of adversity. Complementing this is the Latin phrase. “Ad Astra Per Aspera”, meaning “through hardship to the stars”, embodying the relentless pursuit of excellence through discipline and grit. These qualities, inherent to both warriors and athletes, are woven into the very fabric of this creation With typography interlaced with celestial motifs and our signature snake graphic, this piece is more than just streetwear-it is a tribute to perseverance, championing both the sport and the athletes who define it.
